Iced Latte

Description

An iced latte flavored with vanilla and caramel is a delicious and refreshing pick me up.

While it is a particularly great drink in hot weather, iced lattes are great to drink year round!

Ingredients

  • Ice
  • 2 shots of espresso
  • Milk
  • Caramel syrup
  • Vanilla syrup

Steps

  1. Put a tablespoon of vanilla syrup in the bottom of the glass.
  2. Fill glass with ice.
  3. Pour in espresso shots.
  4. Gently pour milk on the ice to layer the milk over the espresso.
  5. Drizzle with caramel syrup.
