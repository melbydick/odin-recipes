Panang Curry

Description

Panang Curry is a delicious and creamy red curry originating from Malaysia. Kaffir lime leaves are what give this curry a unique flavor.

The curry is typically served with rice and garnished with Thai basil leaves.

Ingredients

  • Panang curry paste
  • Coconut milk
  • Minced garlic
  • Kaffir lime leaves
  • Diced chicken
  • Diced onion

Steps

  1. Saute onion and garlic.
  2. Add curry paste.
  3. Stir in coconut milk.
  4. Add chicken and lime leaves and let simmer 10 minutes.
  5. Serve over rice.
