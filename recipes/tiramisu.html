Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu is a classic Italian dessert layered with creamy filling, coffee soaked ladyfingers, and a generous layer of cocoa powder on the top.

Surprisingly, the authentic way to make this dish is using store bought ladyfingers instead of homemade!

Ingredients

  • Strong coffee
  • Ladyfingers
  • Vanilla marscapone filling
  • Whipped cream
  • Cocoa powder
  • Chocolate shavings

Steps

  1. Spread vanilla filling in the bottom of dish.
  2. Arrange ladyfingers in a single layer on top of the cream.
  3. Generously brush ladyfingers with the strong coffee.
  4. Repeat layers to mostly fill dish
  5. For the top, cover with a layer of whipped cream, topped with cocoa powder and chocolate shavings.
